Labour Chowk – Empowering India’s Construction Workforce


Labour Chowk is India’s first tech-driven hiring platform dedicated to the construction and blue-collar workforce. Founded in 2026, the platform connects daily-wage workers directly with verified contractors and construction companies, eliminating middlemen and ensuring transparency, fair wages, and dignity at work. From masons, electricians, plumbers, painters, helpers to site supervisors, Digital Labour Chowk helps workers find the right jobs at the right sites, while enabling contractors to hire skilled and trusted manpower quickly and efficiently through a single digital platform.
